Roasted Potatoes

Description

Roasted red potatoes at their best — plain and simple. Red potatoes are tossed with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roasted to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds small red new potatoes, halved
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt and freshly ground black pepper
  1. Prepare all ingredients. Set oven to 400°F (200°C) and position the rack at the bottom.
  2. Coat potatoes with oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Arrange them on a baking sheet, cut-side down.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven for 20-30 minutes, until tender and golden. Serve.
